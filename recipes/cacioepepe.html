Cacio e Pepe

Description

Pasta shape of your choice (Preferably Spaghetti or Bucatini)

Ingredients

  • Chosen Pasta Shape
  • Freshly Cracked Black Pepper
  • Saved Pasta Water
  • Freshly Grated Pecorino
  • Unsalted Butter
  • EVOO
  • Salt to Taste

Directions

  1. Simmer EVOO and pepper
  2. Cooka Da Pasta
  3. Add Pasta Water and Butter to the EVOO/Pepper Mixture
  4. Meld
  5. Top With Pecorino & Serve
