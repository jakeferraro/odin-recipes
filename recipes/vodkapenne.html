Vodka Penne

Description

Penne rigate with a "vodka" sauce, created by reducing vodka in a pan and adding cream and crushed San Marzano tomatoes.

Ingredients

  • Penne Rigate
  • San Marzano Tomatoes
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Vodka (Not Tito's)
  • Heavy Cream
  • Freshly Grated Parmesan Cheese
  • Crushed Red Pepper Flakes
  • Salt and Pepper

Directions

  1. Makea Da Sauce
  2. Cooka Da Pasta
  3. Meld
