Rigatoni Bolognese

Description

Rigatoni (Best Pasta Shape) with a traditional bolognese sauce (Meat Sauce)

Ingredients

  • Rigatoni
  • San Marzano Tomatoes
  • Soffritto (Onions, Celery, Carrots)
  • Ground Beef Chuck
  • Italian Red Wine (Preferably a Chianti)
  • EVOO
  • Chicken Stock
  • Freshly Grated Parmesan
  • Tomato Paste
  • Bay Leaf
  • Salt and Pepper

Directions

  1. Cooka Da Beef
  2. Makea Da Sauce
  3. Reduce
  4. Cooka Da Pasta
  5. Meld
